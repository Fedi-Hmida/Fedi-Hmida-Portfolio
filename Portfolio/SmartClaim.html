Project Overview

SmartClaim is an intelligent multichannel platform for automotive insurance claim management developed during a 2‑month internship at ADDINN Tunisia. The solution combines a Flutter mobile application with an AI-powered damage detection service to automate claim analysis and streamline communication between policyholders and claim managers.

Key features include:

  • Secure authentication and comprehensive claim declaration with multimedia attachments
  • AI damage detection using YOLO with real-time processing via FastAPI
  • Real-time claim tracking with status timeline and notifications
  • Integrated messaging and document management

AI Model highlights: Precision 92.9%, Recall 95.0%, mAP@0.5 97.4%, mAP@0.5:0.95 88.8%.
