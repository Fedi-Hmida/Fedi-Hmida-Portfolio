Meet Onboardify

We're proud to unveil Onboardify, a hybrid onboarding solution designed and developed by our team CodeRockers.

What is Onboardify?

Onboardify is a dual-platform system that helps companies deliver a seamless, engaging, and personalized onboarding experience for new employees from day one.

Desktop Application

JavaFX-based solution for secure internal environments

Web Application

Symfony-based platform for flexible, always-on access

Core Features

  • Personalized Onboarding
  • Gamification
  • Real-time Analytics
  • Integrated Communication
